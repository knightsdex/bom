BOMF, the Book of Matt Furie, contains all Matt Furie memes. BOMF is global!





In every generation, there are artists who capture the strange, colorful heartbeat of internet culture. Among them, Matt Furie stands as a modern folk legend — a creator whose characters, humor, and surreal worlds have crossed from underground comics into the living DNA of memes, social movements, and digital subcultures.

The Book of Matt Furie (BOMF) is more than a token — it’s a decentralized archive of that legacy, bound not by paper and ink but by blockchain and community. Inspired by the whimsical, rebellious, and sometimes chaotic spirit of Furie’s creations, BOMF aims to preserve and amplify the raw creativity that fuels online culture, while transforming it into a living, evolving digital artifact.
